### Summary

In this folder, we offer two ways to generate Uniform design.

- `UniformDesign.py` provided by Liangwei Qi, offers one way to generate design in high dimensional space in terms of WD.
- `KTA-UD.R` provided by Ruonan Zheng, offers another way to generate design with different discrepancy criterions. For details, see https://github.com/zhengruonan7/Kronecker-threshold-accepting-KTA-.git
